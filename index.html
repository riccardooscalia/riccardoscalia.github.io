About

Sailing has been central to how Riccardo thinks and decides. Managing a boat under pressure taught him to read conditions carefully, act with composure, and commit when it matters. The same principles guide his approach to markets and analysis.

Riccardo is completing his MSc in Corporate Finance at LUISS University of Rome, following an Erasmus semester at Utrecht University focused on Quantitative Finance. His academic work centres on asset pricing, risk, and quantitative methods — with a thesis on empirical drivers of risk premia in gold and silver markets.

In parallel, he manages a personal investment portfolio as a structured exercise in capital allocation, risk monitoring, and macro thesis formulation. Contemporary art and design inform the same attention to clarity and proportion he brings to financial work.
